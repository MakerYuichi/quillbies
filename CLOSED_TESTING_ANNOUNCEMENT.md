# 🎉 Quillby Closed Testing - Join Now!

## 📱 What is Quillby?

Quillby is your adorable hamster study companion that makes productivity fun! Track your habits, focus on your studies, and watch your virtual hamster thrive as you build better routines.

### ✨ Key Features:
- 🐹 **Virtual Hamster Companion** - Your buddy grows with your productivity
- ⏱️ **Focus Sessions** - Pomodoro-style study timer with rewards
- 💧 **Habit Tracking** - Water, meals, sleep, and exercise
- 🏠 **Room Customization** - Decorate your hamster's room with coins you earn
- 🎨 **Themes** - Unlock beautiful themes as you progress
- 🎯 **Deadlines** - Never miss important tasks
- 📊 **Stats & Streaks** - Track your progress over time
- 💎 **Shop System** - Earn coins and gems to unlock items
